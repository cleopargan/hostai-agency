## What It Is

Most hotel websites convert at 1–2%. That means 98 out of every 100 visitors you paid to acquire leave without booking — many going straight to Booking.com or Expedia.

The Direct Booking AI is a conversion layer we install on your website that intercepts, engages, and converts visitors before they abandon. It combines AI-powered live chat, rate comparison tools, and intelligent booking nudges into a single, seamless experience.

---

## How It Works

### 1. Intent Detection
The AI monitors visitor behaviour in real time — pages viewed, time spent, scroll depth, return visits. When it detects booking intent (e.g. visiting the rates or rooms page), it activates the engagement layer.

### 2. AI Chat Assistant
A trained hotel-specific assistant appears at the right moment with:
- Personalised room recommendations based on browsing behaviour
- Direct booking incentives (best rate guarantee, room upgrades, early check-in)
- Instant answers to common pre-booking questions
- Seamless handoff to a human agent if needed

The assistant is trained on your property, your room types, your policies, and your unique selling points.

### 3. Rate Transparency Widget
A real-time widget that shows visitors your direct rate vs. current OTA prices — powered by a live feed from your booking engine. When your rate is equal or better (which it should be), this eliminates the last reason to book via an OTA.

### 4. Abandoned Booking Recovery
For visitors who start a booking but don't complete it, we trigger:
- On-site exit-intent overlays with a soft incentive
- Email sequences (if email was captured) recovering 8–14% of abandoned sessions
- Retargeting ads across Google and Meta reinforcing the direct booking value

### 5. Smart Urgency Engine
Dynamic, real-time scarcity signals:
- "3 rooms left at this rate"
- "6 people looking at this room right now"
- "This rate expires in [countdown]"

All signals are accurate and pulled from your live inventory — no fake urgency.
