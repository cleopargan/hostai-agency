## What It Is

Most hotel operators are flying blind. They get a Google Ads report here, a Facebook summary there, an OTA dashboard somewhere else — and no way to see the full picture. The CEO Command Center solves that.

It's a live, unified reporting system that pulls data from every marketing channel we manage — Google Ads, Facebook/Instagram, Bing, SEO, direct bookings — and presents it in a single dashboard with one number that matters: **cost per direct booking vs. OTA commission**.

---

## What You See

### The Live Dashboard
A real-time view of every channel, every campaign, and every booking source:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Google Ads ROAS** | Revenue generated per dollar spent (live) |
| **Facebook Cost Per Booking** | What each direct booking costs across Meta channels |
| **Bing CPC** | Cost per click, benchmarked against industry averages |
| **SEO Sessions** | Organic traffic growth, month-over-month |
| **Direct Bookings This Month** | Running total vs. same period last year |
| **OTA Commission Saved** | Dollar value of direct bookings vs. equivalent OTA cost |

### The Weekly Snapshot (Every Monday)
Delivered to your inbox every Monday at 8am in your timezone:
- Top-performing campaigns of the week
- Budget pacing and projected month-end spend
- Any campaigns flagged for review
- One recommended action item

### The Monthly Strategy Report (1st of Month)
A full-picture report every month:
- Channel performance breakdown (ROAS, CPB, CTR, conversions)
- Direct vs. OTA booking split — trending up or down
- SEO ranking movements (all tracked keywords)
- Competitor activity summary
- Next month budget recommendations
- Strategic priorities for the coming 30 days

### The Quarterly Review Call
A 60-minute video call with your account strategist:
- Year-to-date performance vs. goals
- Market analysis and competitive positioning
- Seasonal strategy and budget planning
- Long-term roadmap discussion

## Technology Stack

The Command Center is built on top of:
- **Google Looker Studio** — primary dashboard (shared, always live)
- **Google Analytics 4** — website and booking funnel data
- **Google Ads API** — direct campaign data pull
- **Meta Ads API** — Facebook/Instagram campaign data
- **Microsoft Advertising API** — Bing campaign data
- **Google Search Console** — SEO performance data
- **Custom booking attribution model** — first-click, last-click, and linear attribution views

All data is refreshed every 24 hours. Live campaign metrics update every 4 hours.

## The Single Number That Matters

Every month we calculate one figure for you:

> **Net Revenue Impact** = Total direct bookings revenue − Total NightDesk management fee − Total ad spend

If that number isn't positive and growing, we work for free until it is.
