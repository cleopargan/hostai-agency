## The Guest Journey Automation

### Phase 1 — Post-Booking Confirmation (Day 0)
**Goal:** Reduce cancellations, increase guest confidence

- Branded confirmation email with booking summary
- Property highlights, local area guide, and transport options
- Pre-arrival upsell offer: room upgrade, airport transfer, romantic package
- Link to a "Tell Us About Your Stay" preference form

### Phase 2 — Pre-Arrival Sequence (Day −7 and Day −2)
**Goal:** Maximise ADR, reduce no-shows, set expectations

**Day −7:**
- Reminder email with local events happening during their stay
- Early check-in upsell (revenue: $25–$60 per room)
- F&B package offer (breakfast, dinner, in-room dining)

**Day −2:**
- WhatsApp or SMS check-in reminder
- Real-time weather forecast for their dates
- Final upgrade offer at reduced price (clearing unsold inventory)
- Express check-in form to reduce front desk friction

### Phase 3 — In-Stay Engagement (Day 1+)
**Goal:** Resolve issues before they become reviews, increase spend

- Day 1 welcome message with concierge contact
- Mid-stay satisfaction pulse (1-question NPS)
- Local recommendations sent proactively (restaurants, activities)
- In-room dining / spa / activity prompts based on stay length

### Phase 4 — Post-Stay Review Generation (Check-out + 4 hours)
**Goal:** Capture 5-star reviews at peak satisfaction

- Automated thank-you message 4 hours after check-out
- Direct links to Google, TripAdvisor, and Booking.com reviews
- Smart routing: satisfied guests → public reviews / unhappy guests → private feedback form
- Review response management on all platforms (weekly)

### Phase 5 — Win-Back Campaign (Day +60 and Day +180)
**Goal:** Drive repeat direct bookings

- Personalised "We miss you" email sequence
- Exclusive returning guest rate (direct only, never on OTAs)
- Seasonal offers tied to their original travel pattern
