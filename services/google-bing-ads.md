We manage your entire paid search stack: campaign structure, keyword strategy, bid optimisation, ad copy, Google Hotel Ads, and Bing's often-overlooked inventory. All of it. Fully managed.

## Google Ads

### Search Campaigns
Target travellers actively searching for accommodation in your market. We build tightly themed ad groups with single-keyword intent — so your ads show at the right moment, to the right person, at the right bid.

**What's included:**
- Full keyword research and competitor analysis
- Branded + non-branded campaign separation
- Negative keyword management (ongoing)
- Ad copy writing and A/B testing
- Quality Score optimisation
- Weekly bid adjustments

### Google Hotel Ads (Property Promotion Ads)
Appear directly in Google's hotel search results — the hotel carousels, Maps listings, and price comparison panels. We connect your property management system (PMS) or booking engine to Google's Hotel Center and manage the feed.

**Why it matters:** Hotel Ads show your direct rate vs. OTA rates side-by-side. When your rate is competitive, you win the click — and the commission.

### Display & Remarketing
Re-engage visitors who browsed your site but didn't book. We run display campaigns across the Google Display Network and YouTube to stay front-of-mind throughout the booking consideration window.
